About

1. Operations Manager Responsibilities:
• Oversee daily operations of the logistics side of the business.
• Manage logistics staff and logistics systems.
• Plan routes and process shipments.
• Ensure effective and safe use of warehouse equipment.

2. Human Resources (HR) Manager Responsibilities:
• Recruit, train, and onboard new staff.
• Manage payroll and benefits.
• Ensure compliance with labour laws.
• Handle employee relations and resolve conflicts.

3. Finance Manager Responsibilities:
Oversee financial operations including invoicing, budgeting, and accounting.
Manage company finances, prepare financial statements, and ensure taxes are paid on time.
Analyze financial data and produce forecasts.
Ensure regulatory compliance in financial operations.

4. IT Support/Systems Administrator Responsibilities:
Manage IT infrastructure, from hardware (servers, computers, communication devices) to software (ERP systems, databases).
Ensure data security and backup systems.
Support office staff with IT needs and troubleshoot system issues.
Optimize and maintain logistics software systems.

5. Customer Service Representative Responsibilities:
Handle customer inquiries and complaints.
Provide information about products and services.
Track and manage orders and shipments.
Maintain customer satisfaction and manage feedback.

6. Logistics Coordinator Responsibilities:
Schedule and track shipments and inventory.
Coordinate with vendors, customers, and internal teams.
Handle logistics paperwork (e.g., bills of lading, invoices, orders).
Ensure compliance with laws and regulations pertaining to transportation and logistics.

7. Marketing and Communications Specialist Responsibilities:
Develop and execute marketing strategies.
Manage social media accounts and content.
Produce promotional materials and press releases.
Analyze market trends and adjust strategies accordingly.

8. Compliance Officer Responsibilities:
Ensure that the company follows laws and
regulations applicable to logistics and transportation.
Stay updated on changes in legislation affecting logistics.
Prepare reports and documentation for compliance.
Handle audits and inspections.

9. Data Analyst Responsibilities:
Analyze logistics data to improve efficiency.
Produce reports on logistics performance.
Support decision-making with quantitative analysis.
Forecast demand and logistics requirements.
